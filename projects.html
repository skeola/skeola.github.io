Gloomhaven Companion App

Language: React Native

Repo: GCA

This app was made to assist with the setup and breakdown of the popular board game, Gloomhaven. The game is extremely fun (and time consuming) and the way I describe it to people is "like DnD but without a DM". However, it requires a lot of setup and breakdown and I wanted to both learn a new language (React Native) and make my life easier since I am always the roommate designated for setup/breakdown. Various time commitments have prevented me from completing this project but I do intend on slowly finishing it. Also, there is an official companion app you can find here: Gloomhaven Helper.
